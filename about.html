ABOUT

Lets Go is a platform that records information about travel plans among a group. The platform holds information about the trip before, during, and after. This process simplifies the recording of trip information, congregates information, and summarizes what was done.

WHY USE IT

This platform will elevate your trip as it stores information about the trip before it even happens, as it is happening, and summarizes the trip at the end of the journey. Being able to easily track and record reduces stress for everyone adventuring on the trip.

BENEFITS

  • More relaxed trip
  • Transparent information
  • Updated information
  • Tracks information for everyone
  • Summary gives for a quick wrap up of the trip

HOW IT WORKS

You input into each category the information needed:

  • Packing (who is bringing what, what is still needed)
  • Travel (travel time, distance, elevation warnings, cities of rest)
  • Activities (what it is, location, time, cost)
  • Cost (gas, activities, food, miscellaneous, extra)

In the end it adds everything up for you!
